Our Hair

Brazilian Body Wave

215Hair Virgin Brazilian Signature Wave is our most versatile import. Meaning, once wet all the follicles will began to curl in its natural direction together in the same pattern. This hair can be flat ironed straight and styled to your preference. With the cuticle layer still intact (which is why the hair is Virgin Hair) it can be dyed and or bleached to any desired color. With excellent maintance and proper care this hair can last from 6 to 12 months. For a full head installation we suggest two 3.5oz bundles of hair and for more flowing and vidvid styling we suggest adding a 3rd bundle.

Lengths

20", 22", 24", 26", 28", 30", 32", 34"

Weight

All 215Hair Extensions are manufactured and imported in the standardized weight of 3.5 ounces / 100 grams. Two bundles (7 ounces / 200 grams) is enough for a full head. Three or more for really full style.
