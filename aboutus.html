About 215Hair

215 Hair was founded with a rebellious spirit and a lofty objective: To sale high-quality, long lasting virigin human hair extensions at a revolutionary price point.

215Hair Proprietors / Team

Stanley H. Griggs II

Stanley founded 215Hair (formally Cuteti Hair Company) in 2011 with the goal in mind to disrupt the human hair extension industry, which he believed was and still is overpriced and does not make customers the #1 priority. Using his contacts abroad he was able to create a relationship and a pipeline with an outstanding manufacturer abroad.

215Hair is able to achieve is remarkable pricing with a direct relationship with our husband and wife suppliers who owns the facility where our extensions sewn into track / weft. Also, we import one type of hair to optimize our pricing strategy in which we pass our savings onto you. Customer service is what makes businesses and Cuteti will be #1 in customer service.

Harold Shomo

Harold Shomo 215Hair Partner and Operations. He takes pride in the product and believe that any product sold should be the best of the best when coming from 215Hair. He is in the business for longevity versus competitors who want to make a buck.

He takes interest in your feedback. 215Hair is the most luxurious Brazilian hair extensions available, can be washed, blow dried, curled, dyed, cut, and looks as if it's new. Furthermore customers pride us on our hair that doesn't tangle or shed. Please send your thoughts, we appreciate your feedback. #1 in Customer Service.
